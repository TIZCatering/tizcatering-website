About Tiz Catering

At Tiz Catering, we bring the rich, bold flavors of Nigeria to Calgary. Every dish is prepared with authentic recipes, fresh ingredients, and the kind of love that turns a meal into a celebration.

Whether you are planning a wedding, birthday party, corporate event, or just craving a taste of home, we have you covered. From our signature Jollof Rice to hearty soups and perfectly spiced proteins, every order is made to satisfy.

Based in Calgary, AB, we cater events of all sizes and also offer individual tray orders for everyday enjoyment. Let us bring the flavor to your table.
